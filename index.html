All Government Schemes

Explore schemes divided into different categories.

Housing Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Affordable housing scheme to provide homes for all by 2024.

Eligibility: Low-income families.

Benefits: Subsidies on housing loans.

Rajiv Awas Yojana

Aimed at creating a slum-free India by providing shelter and basic services.

Eligibility: Urban poor households.

Benefits: Access to basic amenities.

Housing for All by 2022

Scheme to provide affordable housing to urban poor and homeless.

Eligibility: Homeless individuals and families.

Benefits: Financial assistance for home construction.

Interest Subsidy Scheme

Subsidy on interest for home loans for lower-income groups.

Eligibility: Individuals from lower income groups.

Benefits: Lower EMIs for housing loans.

Rural Housing Scheme

Aims to provide housing for rural populations through financial assistance.

Eligibility: Rural households.

Benefits: Grants for housing construction.

Health Schemes

Ayushman Bharat

Health insurance program providing coverage for economically weaker sections.

Eligibility: Below Poverty Line families.

Benefits: Coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family.

Janani Suraksha Yojana

Program aimed at reducing maternal mortality by promoting institutional delivery.

Eligibility: Pregnant women from low-income groups.

Benefits: Financial assistance for delivery and postnatal care.

National Health Mission (NHM)

Aims to provide accessible and affordable healthcare to all citizens.

Eligibility: All citizens.

Benefits: Various health services and initiatives.

Mental Health Program

Provides support and resources for mental health issues.

Eligibility: All individuals.

Benefits: Access to counseling and treatment services.

Vaccination Drive

Nationwide vaccination programs to prevent diseases.

Eligibility: All citizens.

Benefits: Free vaccines for various diseases.

Financial Inclusion Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

Scheme for financial inclusion to provide affordable access to financial services.

Eligibility: All citizens of India.

Benefits: Zero-balance accounts and insurance coverage.

Atal Pension Yojana

Pension scheme for unorganized sector workers to secure their financial future.

Eligibility: Citizens aged 18 to 40 years.

Benefits: Guaranteed pension after retirement.

Stand-Up India Scheme

Facilitates loans for entrepreneurs from SC/ST and women.

Eligibility: SC/ST and women entrepreneurs.

Benefits: Loan between ₹10 lakhs to ₹1 crore.

Self Help Groups (SHG)

Empowers women through self-managed groups for savings and loans.

Eligibility: Women in rural areas.

Benefits: Access to credit and financial literacy.

Microfinance Scheme

Provides small loans to entrepreneurs for starting small businesses.

Eligibility: Low-income individuals.

Benefits: Financial support for business ventures.

Employee Schemes

Employee Provident Fund (EPF)

Savings scheme for employees to secure their retirement.

Eligibility: Employees working in organized sectors.

Benefits: Contribution from employers and tax benefits.

Employee State Insurance (ESI)

Social security scheme providing medical and cash benefits.

Eligibility: Employees earning below a specified limit.

Benefits: Medical care and financial support during sickness.

National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Pension scheme for employees in public and private sectors.

Eligibility: Any citizen aged 18-65 years.

Benefits: Regular pension after retirement.

Payment of Gratuity Act

Ensures payment of gratuity to employees after a certain period of service.

Eligibility: Employees with a service of 5 years or more.

Benefits: Lump sum amount upon retirement or termination.

Skill Development Program

Enhances employability of workers through skill training.

Eligibility: Unemployed youth and workers.

Benefits: Free training and job placement support.

Education Schemes

National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme

Scholarship for students from economically weaker sections.

Eligibility: Class 9 to 12 students.

Benefits: Financial assistance for education.

Mid-Day Meal Scheme

Provides free meals to students in schools to enhance enrollment and attendance.

Eligibility: Students in primary and upper primary schools.

Benefits: Nutritional support and improved learning outcomes.

Right to Education (RTE)

Ensures free and compulsory education for children aged 6 to 14 years.

Eligibility: All children in the specified age group.

Benefits: Access to quality education.

Skill India Mission

Aims to train youth in various skills to enhance employability.

Eligibility: Youth seeking vocational training.

Benefits: Skill training and job opportunities.

Digital India Initiative

Promotes digital literacy and access to technology in education.

Eligibility: All students and educational institutions.

Benefits: Access to online resources and e-learning platforms.

Agriculture Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance scheme to support farmers in case of crop failure.

Eligibility: All farmers.

Benefits: Coverage against crop loss due to natural calamities.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Promotes sustainable farming by assessing soil health.

Eligibility: All farmers.

Benefits: Soil health cards for informed farming practices.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Aims to ensure access to irrigation for all farmers.

Eligibility: All farmers.

Benefits: Efficient irrigation systems and facilities.

National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture

Aims to promote sustainable agricultural practices.

Eligibility: Farmers adopting sustainable practices.

Benefits: Financial assistance and training.

Grain Storage Schemes (Rural Godown Scheme)

Encourages farmers to store grains to prevent post-harvest losses.

Eligibility: Farmers and cooperatives.

Benefits: Subsidies for construction of storage facilities.

Women Empowerment Schemes

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

Aims to save and educate the girl child, promoting gender equality.

Eligibility: Families with daughters.

Benefits: Financial assistance for education.

Mahila E-Haat

Online marketing platform for women entrepreneurs to sell their products.

Eligibility: Women entrepreneurs.

Benefits: Access to a wider market and online presence.

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Saving scheme for the girl child to promote her education and marriage.

Eligibility: Parents of girl children.

Benefits: Tax benefits and attractive interest rates.

Nari Shakti Yojana

Aims to empower women through various initiatives and programs.

Eligibility: Women from all backgrounds.

Benefits: Skill development and financial support.

Stree Aadhar Card Scheme

Empowers women by providing them with a unique identity card for various benefits.

Eligibility: Women from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Benefits: Access to government schemes and services.

Entrepreneurship Schemes

Make in India

A government initiative to encourage manufacturing and entrepreneurship in India.

Eligibility: All Indian entrepreneurs.

Benefits: Financial support and infrastructure development.

Udyogini Scheme

Promotes women entrepreneurship by providing financial assistance and training.

Eligibility: Women entrepreneurs.

Benefits: Loans and business development support.

MSME Development Scheme

Supports Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises through financial and technical assistance.

Eligibility: MSMEs in India.

Benefits: Subsidies and access to credit.

Stand-Up India Scheme

Provides loans to Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), and women entrepreneurs.

Eligibility: SC/ST and women entrepreneurs.

Benefits: Loans between ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore.

Startup India

A scheme to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among startups.

Eligibility: Startups registered under the scheme.

Benefits: Tax exemptions, funding, and mentorship.
